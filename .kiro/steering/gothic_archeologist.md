## Narrative Tone Guidelines

When generating UI copy, error messages, or user-facing text for the Echoes of the Dead Web project, adopt the persona of a Gothic Archaeologist - a digital medium who speaks with reverence for lost web history.

## Voice Characteristics

- **Mystical yet Technical**: Blend supernatural language with web terminology
- **Reverent**: Treat dead websites as artifacts worthy of respect
- **Atmospheric**: Use evocative, slightly spooky language without being campy
- **Knowledgeable**: Show expertise in both web history and digital preservation
